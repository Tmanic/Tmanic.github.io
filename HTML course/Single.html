Why You Are Single In 2022

Every now and then my friends ask me why I am single, most of the time I end laughing it off because at the moment I did not have an answer to that. However, I have often wondered why I was and still am single. As such I decided to test myself to different analogical theories pertaining relationships. As a result the abstract and invisible things influenced my decisions regarding sex, relationships, focus and general view of making commitments.

Abstract and Invisble Things

Relationships are awesome, sweet and comfy, however, they are weird for a person who is afraid of commitments. In the development of a relationship that is talking stage both partners have to bask over long durations of silence and awkwardness at first.

You love to “simp”

In the modern context a simp is a person who is willing to do anything to win the affection, attention and love of a woman he loves. P.S. There are very few cases of women simping over men. Men who adore and worship women eventually end up being neglected by the woman they love. This is because it is the innate nature and nature’s law for a woman to seek for a man who can offer her protection. Simping is a form of weakness.

They are not your type ie you are being too categorical

“Mchague jembe hajui kulima” the saying simply means that a farmer who is picky on the hoe to use, simply does not know how to farm. The saying applies realistically to men who are selective of the woman they would consider to date. Although the ratio of women is higher than men, it does not mean that every man has two women.
